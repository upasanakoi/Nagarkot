About Nagarkot

Nagarkot, located just a short drive from Kathmandu, is a popular destination for its panoramic Himalayan views. Known for its tranquil ambiance, it offers an escape from city life into the lap of nature.

Top Attractions

Himalayan Viewpoint

Enjoy the sunrise over the majestic Himalayas, including Mount Everest.

Nature Trails

Explore scenic trails that wind through forests and charming villages.

Nagarkot Tower

Visit the observation tower for a 360-degree view of the surrounding landscapes.
